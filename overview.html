The Bigger Picture

Situated in the middle of peer Catholic universities in terms of undergraduate enrollment, Gonzaga was an ordinary institution at the turn of the century. The unparalleled growth over the first decade of the 21st century has catapulted Gonzaga to one of the top 25 largest Catholic universities in the United States. Simultaneously, Spokane has seen significant growth in employment and average income. While the two are not causal, strong universities enable stable employment and spending. Gonzaga itself is the largest tourist destination in Spokane due to its large student body, national prestige, and basketball appeal — all factors draw significant benefit to broader Spokane and the businesses that reside within it.
